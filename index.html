Welcome

Hello! My name is Alexandra Vignona, and I am entering my second year in the Master of Library and Information Science (MLIS) program at the University of Washington Information School.

This website serves as my Digital Fieldwork (DFW) portfolio, documenting my internship with the Maine Historical Society. Throughout the quarter, I will share weekly reflections and artifacts that highlight my learning, professional development, and contributions to the organization. These experiences support my growth in archival practice, digital collections, and cultural heritage preservation while helping me achieve my DFW learning outcomes.
